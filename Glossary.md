# Glossary

**[Causal Opacity](./Causal Opacity.md)****:** It is impossible to know, for things above a very low bar of complexity, how they got that way or make accurate predictions about them. [^1]
**Defragilizing**: Improving something by removing an element that makes (or may make) it fragile.
**[Legibility](Legibilities.md)**: The forced packaging of information so that an organization can understand it. [^2]
**[MECE](MECE.md)**: Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive. When there’s a list of things that covers a topic, the list is [MECE](MECE.md) if it both covers everything in the topic, and none of the list items overlap. 
**[Via Negativa](./Via Negativa.md)****:** Taking things away. Latin for “negative way”.[^3]
**[Via Positiva](./Via Positiva.md)****:** Adding things. Latin for “positive way”. [^4]
