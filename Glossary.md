**[Causal Opacity](/causal-opacity.html)****:** It is impossible to know, for things above a very low bar of complexity, how they got that way or make accurate predictions about them. [^1]

**Defragilizing**: Improving something by removing an element that makes (or may make) it fragile.

**[Legibility](/legibilities.html)**: The forced packaging of information so that an organization can understand it. [^2]

**[MECE](/mece.html)**: Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive. When there’s a list of things that covers a topic, the list is [MECE](/mece.html) if it both covers everything in the topic, and none of the list items overlap. 

**[Via Negativa](/via-negativa.html)****:** Taking things away. Latin for “negative way”.[^3]

**[Via Positiva](/via-positiva.html)****:** Adding things. Latin for “positive way”. [^4]
