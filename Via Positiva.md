---
title: Via Positiva
permalink: /via-positiva.html
---

"The positive way", it normally means describing something by what it is (in contrast to *[via negativa](/via-negativa.html)*, describing something by what it is not*),* but in an *antifragile* way it means to add something. As a heuristic for analyzing the impact of changing a system, adding something new is inherently unpredictable, so you should proceed with caution and do it experimentally.
